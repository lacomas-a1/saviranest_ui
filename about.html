Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book a stay on SaviraNest?
Booking is simple and takes just a few minutes. Search your desired destination, enter your travel dates and number of guests, then browse our curated listings filtered by price, amenities, and property type. Once you find the perfect match, click "Book Now" to proceed to our secure checkout. Payment is processed instantly via card, mobile money, or bank transfer, and a detailed confirmation — including host contact info, check-in instructions, and a map pin — lands in your inbox within minutes.
What is the cancellation policy?
Most properties offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled check-in, with a full refund processed within 5–7 business days. Some hosts offer flexible same-day cancellations, while others apply a partial refund for shorter notice. The exact policy for each property is prominently displayed on its listing page before you book, so there are never any surprises. If your plans change due to unforeseen circumstances, our support team can also review exception requests on a case-by-case basis.
Are all properties verified?
Absolutely. Every SaviraNest property goes through a rigorous multi-step verification process before appearing on our platform. This includes professional photo validation to ensure listings accurately reflect the space, host identity checks via government-issued ID, cross-referencing of property ownership or rental rights, and a thorough analysis of guest reviews from previous stays. Verified properties carry a blue checkmark on their listing, and our quality team conducts periodic re-audits to maintain standards.
How can I contact support during my stay?
Our 24/7 concierge team is always within reach. You can contact us via live chat directly in the app, email us at support@saviranest.com, or call our dedicated helpline — the number is included in every booking confirmation. For urgent on-site issues like a lockout or maintenance emergency, each reservation also includes a direct line to your host. If the host is unresponsive, our team escalates immediately to ensure your comfort and safety are never compromised.
What amenities can I expect at a SaviraNest property?
Amenities vary by property, but every listing on SaviraNest must meet a baseline standard that includes clean bedding and towels, a functioning kitchen or kitchenette, reliable Wi-Fi, and hot water. Many properties go well beyond the basics — offering pools, private gardens, smart TVs, co-working setups, and local experience kits curated by the host. You can filter search results by any amenity combination, and a full amenities checklist is displayed on every listing so you always know exactly what's included before you book.
Are pets allowed in SaviraNest properties?
Pet policies are set by individual hosts and clearly indicated on each listing with a "Pets welcome", "Pets on request", or "No pets" label. Many of our properties are pet-friendly — especially countryside cottages, villas, and stand-alone homes. When searching, simply use the "Pets allowed" filter to narrow results to suitable options. If you're unsure, you can message the host directly before booking to confirm details such as pet size limits or any additional deposit required. SaviraNest does not charge a platform fee for bringing pets — any pet-related fees are set entirely by the host.

Privacy Policy

Last updated:

SaviraNest is committed to protecting your privacy while delivering a seamless and trusted booking experience. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and the rights you have over your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register, book, or contact support, as well as data generated automatically through your use of our platform:

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, profile photo, and government ID (for host verification)
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, and billing address
  • Booking data: Reservation history, accommodation preferences, travel dates, and guest count
  • Payment data: Encrypted card details, mobile money numbers, and transaction history processed via our PCI-DSS certified payment partners
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and cookies
  • Usage data: Pages visited, searches performed, listings viewed, and time spent on the platform
  • Communications: Messages exchanged with hosts or our support team through the platform

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to deliver, improve, and safeguard our services:

  • To process, confirm, and manage your bookings and reservations
  • To verify host and guest identities and prevent fraudulent activity
  • To personalise property recommendations based on your search history and preferences
  • To process payments securely and issue refunds where applicable
  • To send booking confirmations, check-in reminders, and important account notifications
  • To improve platform performance, resolve technical issues, and develop new features
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • To conduct anonymous analytics and generate aggregated usage statistics

We will never sell your personal data to third parties or use it for purposes beyond those listed above without your explicit consent.

3. Data Sharing

We share your data only where necessary and with trusted parties:

  • Hosts: Your name, profile photo, and contact details are shared with a host only after a booking is confirmed
  • Payment processors: Billing data is passed to our certified payment partners (e.g. Stripe, Flutterwave) solely to process transactions
  • Service providers: Third-party tools used for email delivery, customer support, and analytics operate under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Booking and transaction records are kept for up to 7 years to comply with financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, subject to applicable legal retention requirements.

5. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data at every stage:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data stored at rest
  • Role-based access controls — only authorised personnel can access personal data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automatic session timeouts and multi-factor authentication options

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant regulatory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the incident.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and analyse platform usage. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or our Cookie Policy. Disabling cookies may affect certain platform features.

7. International Transfers

SaviraNest operates across multiple regions. If your data is transferred outside your country of residence, we ensure equivalent protection through standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions recognised under applicable data protection law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing purposes
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

SaviraNest is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by email or a prominent notice on our platform at least 14 days before they take effect.

11. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: privacy@saviranest.com
  • Support: support@saviranest.com
Your trust is at the heart of everything we do at SaviraNest. We are committed to being transparent, responsible, and respectful with your personal information.

Cancellation Policy

Last updated:

We understand that plans can change. SaviraNest's cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both guests and hosts. Please review the terms below carefully before booking, as they determine your eligibility for a refund should you need to cancel.

1. Flexible Bookings

Listings marked as "Flexible" allow guests to cancel at no cost up to 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time. After that window, a one-night fee applies and the remainder of the booking amount is refunded in full. Flexible listings are clearly tagged on the search and listing pages.

2. Standard Cancellation Policy

For listings operating under our standard policy, the following refund schedule applies based on how far in advance the cancellation is made relative to check-in:

  • More than 48 hours before check-in: Full refund of the total booking amount, including the service fee
  • 24 to 48 hours before check-in: 50% refund of the accommodation cost; the service fee is non-refundable
  • Less than 24 hours before check-in: No refund — the full booking amount is retained
  • No-show (failure to check in without notice): No refund and the reservation is automatically cancelled

3. Strict Policy

Some premium or high-demand listings operate under a strict cancellation policy. Under this policy:

  • Cancellations made more than 7 days before check-in receive a 50% refund
  • Cancellations made within 7 days of check-in are non-refundable
  • The applicable policy is clearly displayed on the listing before booking is confirmed

4. Property-Specific Rules

Individual hosts may apply custom cancellation terms that differ from the standard tiers above. Where this is the case, the host's policy takes precedence and is displayed prominently on the listing page. Always review the specific policy of the property you are booking before completing your reservation.

5. How to Cancel

To cancel a booking, log into your SaviraNest account, navigate to "My Bookings", select the relevant reservation, and click "Cancel Booking". You will be shown the refund amount you are eligible for before confirming the cancellation. Cancellations cannot be reversed once confirmed, so please review carefully before proceeding.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days from the date of cancellation confirmation. Funds are returned via the original payment method used at checkout. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or mobile money provider:

  • Credit/debit cards: 5–10 business days
  • Mobile money (e.g. M-Pesa, Airtel Money): 1–3 business days
  • Bank transfers: 7–10 business days

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.

7. Extenuating Circumstances

In exceptional situations beyond a guest's control — such as a serious medical emergency, natural disaster, or government-imposed travel restriction — SaviraNest may offer a full refund or credit outside the standard policy. To apply, contact our support team within 48 hours of your scheduled check-in with supporting documentation (e.g. medical certificate, official government notice). Each case is reviewed individually and our team aims to respond within 2 business days.

8. Host Cancellations

If a host cancels a confirmed booking, the guest will receive a full refund including all service fees. SaviraNest will also assist the affected guest in finding alternative accommodation of equal or greater value at no additional charge where possible. Hosts who cancel confirmed bookings may face penalties including reduced listing visibility, financial penalties, or account suspension depending on the frequency and circumstances of the cancellation.

9. Modifications vs. Cancellations

If you wish to change your travel dates rather than cancel outright, you may submit a modification request through "My Bookings". Date changes are subject to host approval and property availability. If the modified dates result in a lower booking value, the difference will be refunded. If they result in a higher value, the additional amount will be charged at the time of confirmation.

If you have any questions about your specific booking's cancellation terms, please contact our support team at support@saviranest.com before cancelling.
Cookie Policy

Last updated:

This Cookie Policy explains how SaviraNest uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our mobile application. By continuing to use our platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owners. Cookies do not contain viruses and cannot access other files on your device. Similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage may also be used for comparable purposes.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

SaviraNest uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: These are essential for the platform to function. They enable core features such as logging in, maintaining your session, completing a booking, and accessing secure areas. These cookies cannot be disabled without significantly affecting platform functionality.
  • Performance & analytics cookies: These cookies collect anonymous data about how visitors interact with our platform — such as which pages are visited most often and how users navigate between sections. This information helps us identify issues and improve the overall experience. We use tools such as Google Analytics for this purpose.
  • Functional cookies: These cookies remember choices you make on the platform — such as your preferred language, currency, or saved search filters — so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit.
  • Targeting & advertising cookies: These cookies track your browsing behaviour across our platform and, where applicable, across third-party sites. They are used to show you relevant property recommendations and promotional content. We work with select advertising partners who may also set cookies on our behalf.
  • Social media cookies: If you use social sign-in (e.g. via Google or Facebook) or share content from SaviraNest, cookies from those platforms may be placed on your device. These are governed by the respective platform's privacy and cookie policies.

3. How Long Do Cookies Last?

Cookies are either session-based or persistent:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted automatically when you close your browser. Used for login sessions and booking flows.
  • Persistent cookies: These remain on your device for a set period (typically between 30 days and 2 years) or until you manually delete them. Used for remembering preferences and enabling analytics over time.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our platform are set by third-party services we integrate with, including:

  • Google Analytics — for usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Stripe / Flutterwave — for secure payment processing
  • Intercom / Zendesk — for live chat and customer support functionality
  • Meta Pixel — for advertising measurement and retargeting (where applicable)

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review. SaviraNest does not control the cookies placed by these services beyond what is required to integrate their functionality.

5. Managing & Controlling Cookies

You have several options for controlling cookies:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or receive warnings before cookies are set. Instructions vary by browser — refer to your browser's help documentation for guidance.
  • Cookie consent banner: When you first visit SaviraNest, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies through our cookie consent tool. You can revisit your preferences at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.
  • Opt-out tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. For advertising cookies, visit youronlinechoices.com.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the platform, including your ability to complete bookings or retain saved preferences.

6. Cookies and Children

SaviraNest does not knowingly use cookies to track minors under the age of 18. If you believe a child is using the platform without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our technology, legal requirements, or business practices. Any significant changes will be communicated via a notice on our platform. Your continued use of SaviraNest following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

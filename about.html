Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book a stay on SaviraNest?
Simply search for your desired location, select dates and guests, browse curated listings, and click "Book Now". Our secure payment system processes your reservation instantly, and you'll receive a confirmation email within minutes.
What is the cancellation policy?
Most stays offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Specific policies vary by property and are clearly displayed on each listing page.
Are all properties verified?
Yes! Every SaviraNest property undergoes a thorough verification process including photo validation, host identity checks, and guest review analysis.
How can I contact support during my stay?
Our 24/7 concierge team is available via phone, email, or live chat. Each booking includes a dedicated support line for immediate assistance.

Privacy Policy

SaviraNest Privacy Commitment

We collect personal information only to process bookings, improve stay experiences, and communicate offers. Your data is never shared with third parties without consent. We use industry encryption to protect payment details. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

For any privacy inquiries, contact info@saviranest.co.ke.

Booking Terms & Conditions

Reservations & Payments: A deposit of 30% is required to confirm any booking. Remainder due 14 days before arrival. Cancellations made 30+ days before arrival receive full refund minus admin fee. Cancellations within 14 days are non-refundable. Early check-in/late check-out subject to availability. Guests are responsible for their belongings.

We reserve the right to modify bookings due to unforeseen events. Full terms provided upon confirmation.

Cancellation Policy

Flexible cancellations: Free cancellation up to 30 days before check-in. 50% refund between 15-29 days. No refund for cancellations within 14 days of arrival. For groups larger than 6 rooms, different terms apply – please contact us directly. In case of a no-show, total amount is charged.

In rare events like natural disasters, we offer travel credit or rescheduling at no extra cost.

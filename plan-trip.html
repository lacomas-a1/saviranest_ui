7-Day Masai Mara & Lake Nakuru Safari

Day 1-2: Nairobi → Masai Mara. Afternoon game drive.

Day 3-4: Full day game drives, Mara River (witness crossings if season).

Day 5: Drive to Lake Nakuru – flamingos & rhinos.

Day 6: Morning game drive, return to Nairobi.

Includes: 4x4 jeep, full board, park fees, expert guide. From $1,850 p/p sharing.

10-Day Safari & Diani Beach Combo

Highlights: Amboseli elephants, Tsavo West, then fly to Diani. Includes 5 nights beach resort, safari lodges, domestic flights available.

Perfect for honeymooners & families. Prices start at $2,450 per person.

5-Day Watamu & Lamu Cultural Escape

Stay at eco-lodges in Watamu, snorkel at Marine Park, then fly to Lamu. Explore UNESCO heritage, dhow sunset cruise. From $950 p/p.
